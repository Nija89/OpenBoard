Welcome to Open Board

Open Board allows users to sign up, log in, and create posts with a title and description. Users have the option to set the visibility of their posts as either **public** or **private**. Public posts can be viewed by other users, while private posts are only visible to the author.

The site features three main roles: **Member**, **Moderator**, and **Admin**.

  • Member: Members can create posts, update their posts, and delete them. They have full control over their own content.
  • Moderator: Moderators can delete inappropriate posts created by members. They help maintain the integrity and appropriateness of the platform.
  • Admin: Admins have access to all content, including the ability to manage users and delete any user accounts. They have complete control over the website's operations and user management.

With these roles in place, we aim to provide a dynamic and engaging platform where users can share content with different privacy levels and interact with others in meaningful ways.
