Welcome to Our Website

The website will allow users to sign up, log in, and create posts with a title and description. Users will have the option to set the visibility of their posts as either public or private. Public posts can be viewed by other users, who can interact with them by liking or disliking them, while private posts will only be visible to the author. Additionally, the site will feature a dedicated page that showcases the top 10 posts, ranked based on the number of likes and dislikes they receive from other users. This ensures a dynamic and engaging experience, allowing users to share content with varying levels of privacy and interact with others in meaningful ways.
